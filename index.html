Our mission

The dawn of a new day brings about the tough challenge of facing the anxieties and fears particular to our lives, thus, making it difficult to pursue our goals and dreams. Life may as well be a continuous battle with the self, a never-ending concert with badly tuned instruments taking place in our minds. Therefore, in the bustling city of Cluj-Napoca, we found it necessary to create a place where a few moments of inner peace can be achieved by serving tea in the Japanese way. Hence, our name: 
安定 ("antei") bearing the meaning of "stability" or "equilibrium", combined with 区 ("ku"), which stands for "ward" ー "a peaceful area", paints the image of the place we envision.
