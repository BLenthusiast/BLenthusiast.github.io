Experience the Japanese Tea Ceremony with us 

In the world of tea, there's no other event as graceful and sophisticated as the Tea Ceremony of the Rising Sun Country. Practiced by the Japanese for centuries, this ceremony is meant to bring inner harmony and peace within oneself.
We occasionally organize this intimate moment at our teahouse and it is done by natives who trained for decades in the arts of tea.
A kimono or yukata will be provided for you during this process, as well as a brief training before the ceremony.
We kindly ask our clients who want to take part to let us know in advance as well as to pay the fee of 20 RON. The seats are limited, so make sure you sign up as soon as possible!
